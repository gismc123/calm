Add to Home Screen

Calm Down works like a native app when installed on your phone. No app store needed.

Your privacy is fully protected. No data is ever saved, recorded, or transmitted by this app. Everything you write stays only in your current tab and is gone when you close it — unless you choose to download it yourself.

iPhone / iPad (Safari)

  1. Open this page in Safari (not Chrome)
  2. Tap the Share icon at the bottom of the screen
  3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Tap "Add" in the top right corner

Android (Chrome)

  1. Open this page in Chrome
  2. Tap the three-dot menu (⋮) in the top right
  3. Tap "Add to Home Screen" or "Install App"
  4. Confirm by tapping "Add"

Terms of Use

Please read these terms before using Calm Down.

Not a Medical Service

Calm Down is a personal wellness tool designed to support stress management and emotional regulation. It is not a medical service, mental health treatment, or clinical intervention. Nothing in this app constitutes medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Not a Substitute for Professional Care

If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, please contact a qualified professional or call/text 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline). This app is not a replacement for therapy, counseling, or any other professional care.

Use at Your Own Discretion

The tools and exercises in this app are provided as general wellness resources. Results vary by individual. You are responsible for determining whether any tool is appropriate for your situation.

No Warranty

This app is provided "as is" without warranty of any kind. We make no guarantees about outcomes, availability, or fitness for any particular purpose.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is a core principle of this app — not an afterthought.

We collect absolutely nothing. No account, no login, no tracking.

No Data Collection

Calm Down does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Your stress levels, written responses, and session activity never leave your device.

Session Data

All session data lives only in your browser tab. When you close or refresh the tab, everything is gone. Nothing is written to cookies, local storage, or any server.

Downloads

If you choose to download your responses, that file is saved only to your device using your browser's standard download function. We never see it.

No Analytics or Third Parties

This app contains no analytics scripts, ad trackers, or third-party services of any kind. There is nothing here watching you.
